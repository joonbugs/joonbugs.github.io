About Me

I'm currently an undergraduate senior at the School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University, majoring in Artificial Intelligence, and minoring in Human-Computer Interaction. My studies are supported by CMU's Presidential Scholarship.
My research interests and work covers the intersection of accessibility, disability studies, applications of machine learning methods, and effective modalities of conveying and organizing information.
